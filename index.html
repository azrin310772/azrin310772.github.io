    Chargenow is a service from BMW, operated by ChargEV which enables customers in the Malaysia to access the largest network of public charging stations nationwide with a single card - the BMW ChargeNow card.

    ChargEV is owned by Malaysian Green Technology Corporation (GreenTech Malaysia). ChargEV has installed more than 100 charging stations across Malaysia (as of October 2016). At the International Greentech & Eco Products Exhibition & Conference Malaysia (IGEM 2016), BMW Group Malaysia officially announced its partnership with GreenTech Malaysia, where chargEV is to provide BMW plug-in cars with charging infrastructure and facilities across Malaysia.
